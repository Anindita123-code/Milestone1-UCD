Why Make Art?

Art is that one thing which grows with us from our childhood.
It is always within us waiting for us to look back at it, pause and spend some time with it.
In complex times of life, art helps us unwind; frees our creative self and encourages us to play with colors, forms and depth.
It invites us to loosen ourselves into a different world, and yet stay connected.

What is More ?

Art makes our soul happy, it gives confidence. It can win you loads of appreciation and can support you financially as well.
The true essence of creating an art is to open up and feel proud about it.
In "Arts & More" we will be providing you the opportunity to display your work of art to a worldwide audience. In this journey of art, you will also be able to connect with like minded people, exchange and enhance your skills and get back much more. Help us to build a community to explore Art...
