Why Make Art ?

brushes

Art is that one thing which runs with us as child. It is always in there waiting for us to look back at it, pause and spend some time with it. It helps us unwind and frees our cretive self and brings us back to the times where experimenting with colors, forms and depth lets us loos ourself into a different world.

What is more ?

pallete

Art is created for bringing happiness in our souls. It is created to win appreciation. The true essence of creating art is to nurture happy souls and reach your body of work to the right audience. Here in "Arts & More" we will be providing you the opportunity to display our artwork to a worldwide audience. As an artist you will be able to connect with like minded people, exchange and enhance your skills and get back much more. Come Join Us!
